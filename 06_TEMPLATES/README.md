06_TEMPLATES

Purpose

This folder stores reusable templates for KENHU content creation.

Templates save time by providing a consistent starting point for future work.

Contents

- Thumbnail Templates
- Storyboard Templates
- Script Templates
- Caption Templates
- Product Page Templates
- Documentation Templates

Rules

- Keep templates generic and reusable.
- Update templates when a better version is proven.
- Do not mix templates with finished projects.
- Each template should be easy to copy and adapt.
